    • The fall of an empire



    Back in the end of the 12th centuary, the emperor had all the power in Japan.
    He was the direct descendent of the god of the sun Amateratsu.
    But his vassals did not like how he ran the country and so in the beginning iof the 13th centuary, a massive civil war erupted.



    An island or a puzzle



    With the fall of the empire, the land of japan became broken up like a puzzle between all the emerging clans.
    It became known as the period of the warring states, or Sengoku Jidai
    It became a race of wich clan could become the strongest and call himself the Shogun.
    For whoever held the title of shogun, held the control over Japan.
